# MRPeasy – Smart Manufacturing ERP & Production Planning Tool

**MRPeasy** is a powerful, flexible manufacturing resource planning solution designed to help small and medium manufacturers organize production, track inventory, and achieve on-time delivery goals.  
With **MRPeasy**, you can transform chaotic shop floor workflows into structured production schedules.  
**MRPeasy** supports smart scheduling, procurement planning, and real-time job tracking, making it an essential productivity companion for production managers, shop floor supervisors, and business owners.

---

## About MRPeasy

**MRPeasy** combines the simplicity of a production checklist with the power of a full ERP system. Unlike basic inventory tools, **MRPeasy** offers deep customization: custom BOMs, routing steps, work centers, and purchase planning.  
**MRPeasy** works seamlessly with your existing accounting system (QuickBooks, Xero, Zapier) and provides real-time updates across all devices.  
Whether you manage make-to-order production or batch processing, **MRPeasy** adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.  
**MRPeasy** respects your privacy – all production data stays under your control with optional cloud or on-premise deployment.  
With **MRPeasy**, you gain clarity, reduce stockouts, and complete more production orders every day.

## Key Features of MRPeasy

- **Production Scheduling** – **MRPeasy** automatically plans job start and end dates.  
- **Inventory Tracking** – **MRPeasy** tracks raw materials, WIP, and finished goods.  
- **BOM Management** – Create multi-level bills of materials inside **MRPeasy**.  
- **Purchase Planning** – **MRPeasy** generates purchase suggestions based on demand.  
- **Work Order Management** – Create, assign, and track work orders with **MRPeasy**.  
- **Capacity Forecasting** – See load on each work center inside **MRPeasy**.  
- **Lot & Serial Tracking** – Trace finished products back to raw materials using **MRPeasy**.  
- **Bin Location Management** – Organize warehouse storage inside **MRPeasy**.  
- **Sales Order Integration** – Convert sales orders directly into production orders with **MRPeasy**.  
- **Manufacturing Analytics** – View OEE, lead times, and defect rates inside **MRPeasy**.  
- **Mobile Shop Floor App** – Report production progress using **MRPeasy** on any device.  
- **Barcode Scanning** – Receive materials and report finished goods via **MRPeasy**.  
- **MRP Calculation** – Run material requirements planning inside **MRPeasy**.  
- **Supplier Management** – Track vendor performance and pricing in **MRPeasy**.  
- **Export & Backup** – Export your **MRPeasy** data to CSV, Excel, or PDF.
